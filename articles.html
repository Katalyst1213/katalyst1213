Peptide Reconstitution: A Beginner's Guide

Correct reconstitution is critical for maintaining the integrity of lyophilized peptides. This guide covers the importance of using Bacteriostatic Water...

The Process: Gently inject the Bacteriostatic Water (BAC) against the side of the glass vial. Do not spray directly onto the lyophilized powder. The "Gentle Swirl": Avoid shaking the vial, as this can shear the delicate peptide chains. Instead, gently swirl until the solution is clear.

Storage: Once reconstituted, most peptides should be stored in a refrigerator between 2°C and 8°C. Protect the vial from direct sunlight to prevent degradation.

Comparing GLP-1 and GIP Agonists

Understanding the difference between Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, and the new Retatrutide for research purposes...

The Evolution: Semaglutide is a single GLP-1 agonist. Tirzepatide added a second pathway (GIP), while Retatrutide is a "Triple Agonist" (GLP-1, GIP, and Glucagon).

Why it matters: In research models, the triple-action pathway of Retatrutide has shown significantly higher metabolic signaling efficiency. This makes it the current "gold standard" for metabolic research enthusiasts.
